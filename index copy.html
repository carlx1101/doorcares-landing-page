Why Regular Check?

As a landlord, why should your rental property undergo regular inspection?

  • Regular checks are important, in monitoring the condition of your units. This can potentially increase your income, save you time and reduce hassle during the tenancy period.
  • Periodic checks ensure that your property is being properly maintained througout the rental period.
  • Regular checks prevent damagas and defects from worsening, if left unnoticed and unchecked .

About Us



Our mission is simple. We help landlords to check their rented properties thoroughly when tenants move-in and move-out, and in-between, we conduct interim checks too.  It is our strong belief that these checks, when done properly and professionally, eventually lead to a smoother and more hassle-free tenancy. Let DOORCARES be your eyes and ears, as we specialize in checking and identifying all matters pertinent to your valued properties!

Our audits culminate in a documentation of our observations in easily understandable reports, supported by records and photos.  Enjoy peace of mind with DOORCARES, knowing that your properties and possessions are properly accounted for, and that you are well updated on their conditions. All of these, done without you having to be physically present, thus saving you time and hassle.
